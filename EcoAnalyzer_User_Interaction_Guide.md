# 🧭 EcoAnalyzer: User Interaction & Role Management Guide

This guide provides a detailed walkthrough of how to navigate EcoAnalyzer, how different user roles operate, and how to interact with every section of the platform.

---

## 👥 1. User Roles & Permissions

EcoAnalyzer has two distinct roles that define what a user can see and do:

### A. Registered User (`user`)
*The standard role for individuals committed to environmental tracking.*
- **Analyze**: Access the AI engine to calculate carbon footprints.
- **History**: View and search through past analyses.
- **Learning Hub**: Read community articles and take the Eco-Quiz.
- **Contributor Rights**: Write new articles for the community.
- **Self-Management**: Update or request deletion of their own articles.
- **Gamification**: Earn points, badges, and appear on the leaderboard.

### B. Administrator (`admin`)
*The oversight role responsible for platform integrity.*
- **All User Features**: Admins can use the platform exactly like a user.
- **Automatic Publishing**: Articles written by admins are published instantly without review.
- **Moderation Queue**: Access to the specialized **Admin Dashboard** to review all community submissions.
- **Content Control**: Power to approve, reject, or permanently delete *any* article on the platform.
- **Comparison Tool**: Ability to view "Side-by-Side" updates to ensure proposed changes are accurate.

---

## 🗺️ 2. Navigation & Interface Layout

The **Top Navigation Bar** is your primary way to move between features:
- **Home**: Overview of the platform's mission and AI technology.
- **Analyze**: Your workspace for environmental impact calculations.
- **Learning**: The library of community articles and the Eco-Quiz.
- **Leaderboard**: Global rankings of top eco-contributors.
- **About**: Detailed technical stack and mission information.
- **Admin (Admins Only)**: Access to the Review Queue and Moderation tools.

---

## 🧪 3. Detailed Feature Interactions

### 🔍 A. The Analyze Tab
1. **Input Field**: Type a description of a product (e.g., "Apple iPhone 15") or a lifestyle choice (e.g., "Commuting 20km by train daily").
2. **Execution**: Click **"Start Analysis"**.
3. **AI Feedback**: Wait 3-5 seconds for Google Gemini to process.
4. **The Results**:
   - **Footprint Score**: A gauge from 0 (Perfect) to 100 (Critical).
   - **Detailed Breakdown**: Scientific reasoning for the score.
   - **Eco-Tips**: Personalized suggestions for reduction.

### 📚 B. The Learning Hub
- **Article Library**: Browse cards containing titles and summaries. Click **"Read More"** to view full content.
- **Write Article**: Use the **"Create New"** button to open the editor.
  - *Interaction:* Enter a Title and Markdown-supported Content. 
  - *Submit:* Your article enters the Review Queue (unless you are an admin).
- **Eco-Quiz**: Click **"Take the Quiz"**.
  - *Interaction:* Answer 10 randomized scientific questions.
  - *Feedback:* Get instant point rewards and badge unlocks.

### ⚙️ C. User Settings & "My Articles"
Located by clicking your **Profile Icon** in the Navbar:
- **Profile Tab**: Update your name or change your password.
- **My Articles Tab**: A dedicated dashboard for your contributions.
  - **Status Badge**: Shows if your article is 'Approved' or 'Pending'.
  - **Request Edit**: Opens a modal to submit an update request (grayed out if a deletion is pending).
  - **Delete Request**: Flags your article for removal by an admin.
  - **Undo Request**: Cancel a pending deletion before an admin sees it.

### 🛡️ D. Admin Dashboard (Admins Only)
1. **Review Queue**: Lists all pending submissions.
   - **Approve**: Publishes the content immediately.
   - **Reject**: Removes the request.
   - **Side-by-Side Review**: For update requests, view the "Current Content" next to "Proposed Content" to spot changes easily.
2. **Manage Published**: A global search bar to find any live article.
   - **Global Delete**: Permanently removes content without needing a user's request.

## 📊 4. Data Interpretation
- **Green Scores (0-30)**: Low impact, sustainable choices.
- **Orange Scores (31-70)**: Moderate impact, look for alternatives.
- **Red Scores (71-100)**: High impact, significant environmental cost.
- **Points**: Earned through the Quiz (100pts per correct answer) and Article approval (500pts).
